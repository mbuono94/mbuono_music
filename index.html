Released in September 2016, Slow it Down is a five song EP featuring rock, pop, and new wave sounds.

Michael Buono is a singer/songwriter from Arlington, Virginia.

A classically trained pianist turned romantic, Michael began writing songs in high school in the basement of his childhood home. Eight years later, he has just released his first studio album, Slow it Down.
