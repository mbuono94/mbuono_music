Slow it Down coming soon to iTunes, Amazon, and Spotify

Set to be released in December 2016, Slow it Down is a five song EP featuring rock, pop, and new wave sounds.

Michael Buono is a singer/songwriter based in Oakland, California.

A classically trained pianist turned romantic, Michael began writing songs in high school in the basement of his childhood home. Eight years later, he is on the verge of releasing his first studio album, Slow it Down.
